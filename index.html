2012 Colorado Medicare Payment Data

Click on the map to see a visual representation of that area's Medicare payment data.

This visualization shows the amount of money healthcare specialties received from Medicare in 2012. The total payments received are grouped by specialty within cities along the Front Range of Colorado. This is a small subset of the data made public by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services in April 2014.
